No Metro disruptions

When we are running a good service the status will show as ‘No Metro disruptions’. Any delays (less than 15 minutes) to service will be announced via the station PA system and passenger information boards. If delays occur resulting in trains running more than 15 minutes later than advertised the status will be updated by an automatic travel alert system.

5 February 2017 - No trains South Gosforth - Heworth until about 6pm

On Sunday 5 February no trains will run between South Gosforth and Heworth in either direction until about 6pm, to allow for maintenance work. During the work a replacement bus service number 900 will call at or close to all station between Regent Centre and Heworth. Your journey will take longer so please leave extra travelling time.
